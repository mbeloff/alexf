Services

We provide full spectrum service from concept design to completion of individualised, bespoke and luxury furniture. Using only the finest quality materials and tools, our highly qualified collection of tradespeople allows us to provide unique furniture solutions to suit any requirements.

Consultancy

In the initial stages of a project our team will conduct an in house consult with you to measure the space and to work with you to establish a style and basis of what you are looking for and what best suits your needs.

Design

After establishing our working criteria our in-house team of furniture and interior designers will set to work creating furniture specifically and carefully tailored to you. Our design team will be available to assist and inform you right the way through the process from design concept to completion.

Manufacture & Installation

Once the design has been finalised it will go over to manufacture, at which point our carefully selected tradespeople will set about bringing your furniture to life. Once completed our team will also be on hand to oversee all delivery and installation to ensure that everything right up to completion is finished to the highest of standards.

Restoration

Do you have an old or antique piece that you would love to see restored to its former glory, speak to our team about the restoration services we are able to provide.
